What are Interviews

Interviews are formal conversations or interactions between employers and candidates as part of the hiring process. They serve as a means for employers to assess the qualifications, skills, experience, and fit of candidates for a particular job position. Interviews can take various formats such as in-person, phone, or video, and typically involve a series of questions and discussions to evaluate the candidate's suitability. The purpose of interviews is to gather information, assess the candidate's abilities, and make informed decisions on selecting the best candidate for the job.
