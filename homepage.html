Helping students build a better future for themselves.....
Our mission is simple: To build an educational platform that serves as a one-stop-shop for students seeking to enhance their skills and advance their careers. And we made it. CAREER NAVIGATOR is an educational platform exclusively for coding enthusiasts. This platform offers a wide range of resources that you can use for free to level up your coding game. But...But...I don't know anything about coding; what should I do? Just go to the NEWBIE block, and you can move from nothing to something in a few minutes.

Out of the blue, the teachers split us into groups, and they said this group was for a mini project. And that's how this team formed. Everyone started brainstorming, and after a day of debate, we decided to build an educational platform and that's how CARRER NAVIGATOR was created.
Basically, we want to help students who just got into BTech (or are already in BTech) and don't know anything about coding or want to learn how to code. That's what we're focusing on when building this platform. We are students from VIIT, CSE. And currently, we are in our second year, section B. We are dedicating our time and effort to ensure that the platform is of the finest possible quality and fulfills our expectations of being beneficial to our peers and the entire college.
